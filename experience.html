KTU Special

Python Application Developer

As a freelance Python Application Developer for KTU Special, I specialized in building robust automation tools using a Test-Driven Development (TDD) approach to ensure high reliability and code quality. By combining a user-friendly GUI with a rigorously tested back-end, I delivered scalable solutions that empowered non-technical staff to handle complex batch operations with precision and speed.

Assistrend

Social Media Content Creator

During my time at Assistrend, I worked on creating informative and emotionally engaging video content for their Instagram page, with a strong focus on simplifying complex topics for a general audience. One of my key projects was an educational short film about an aspiring young girl who becomes fascinated by astronomy after seeing the night sky, and a boy who patiently explains the universe to her in a relatable, story-driven way. I handled the complete video pipeline, from refining the narrative concept and planning the shots to editing the footage, adding motion graphics, and syncing visuals with music to maintain viewer attention. The goal of this piece was to inspire curiosity about space in younger audiences while aligning with Assistrend’s brand identity as a modern, knowledge-focused platform. Through careful pacing, clear on-screen text, and smooth transitions, the video was designed to feel both cinematic and informative, making complex astronomical ideas feel accessible and emotionally resonant for Instagram viewers.​
